About Cartoons

A cartoon is typically a drawing or animated film in a humorous or exaggerated style. Originally, in the Middle Ages, cartoons referred to preparatory drawings for artwork like paintings or tapestries. By the 19th century, it came to describe humorous art in magazines like Punch. Today, cartoons are used for satire, humor, political commentary, and animations. Cartoonists create still images, while animators bring those images to life in motion.
